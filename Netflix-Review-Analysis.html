Summary

Goal

This analysis of Netflix reviews identified key themes in negative feedback linked to subscription cancellations. The findings highlight recurring dissatisfaction drivers, offering actionable insights to improve customer retention and satisfaction

Process

- Ensured completeness of key columns (content, score, appVersion) and removed duplicates

- Standardized review text for effective analysis by cleaning irrelevant and redundant data

- Added a Topic Label column to categorize reviews by dominant themes

- Created Sentiment Score and Review Length metrics to identify patterns in complaints

Insights

- Technical Complaints Dominate: Topics 1 and 2 made up the majority of reviews, showing critical app performance issues

- Content Matters: Content dissatisfaction was a leading cause of low sentiment in specific regions

- Engagement Correlation: Longer, more detailed reviews often pointed to unresolved billing or app problems

- Yearly Trends: Increasing technical complaints suggest rising user expectations for app reliability
